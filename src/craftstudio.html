    / CONTENT CREATION

    "

    Our project began with crafting jewelry from wax, meticulously sculpting and shaping each piece with precision and care. Our primary focus was to document the journey from the wax creation stage through the silver casting process, ultimately showcasing the jewelry in use. These images offer a glimpse into the transformative process of shaping wax into silver, highlighting the art of creation.

    In the post-production phase, we edited each photograph using Adobe Photoshop. This step ensured that all images adhered to a consistent visual style.

    / WOO COMMERSE

    "

    In our pursuit of delivering a good online shopping experience, we made use of WooCommerce to establish a fully functional webshop on our website. This decision was underpinned by a thorough understanding of user behavior and preferences. Before WooCommerce was integrated, we conducted comprehensive user surveys to gain insights into what constitutes an effective and user-friendly purchasing journey.

    / BRAND & AWARENESS

    "

    To establish brand awareness, we strategically crafted official company profiles on various social media platforms. Take Instagram, for instance, where our focus was to encapsulate the entire Craft Studio universe. We aimed to provide a holistic view of Craft Studio, showcasing not just the products, but also the people and processes behind the brand. Through a combination of engaging videos and images, we offered a glimpse into the artistry of jewelry making, from the initial concept to the final product in everyday use.

    Our goal was to create a compelling narrative that resonated with our audience, allowing them to connect with the essence and authenticity of Craft Studio.
